=================
Log In with Atlas
=================

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 2
   :class: singlecol

Log in to Relational Migrator using your MongoDB Atlas account. When you log 
in with your Atlas account, you can access your MongoDB Atlas cluster and 
use Relational Migrator's :ref:`Query Converter <rm-query-converter>`.

About this Task
---------------

To log in with your Atlas account, Relational Migrator must be running on 
localhost under ports 
- 8278, 
- 8080, 
- or 443. 

If Relational Migrator runs on any other ports, the Atlas log in feature is 
disabled.

Steps
-----

1. Click the :guilabel:`Log in` button on the Relational Migrator home screen.

2. Click :guilabel:`Yes, log in with Atlas`.

3. Provide your Atlas credentials.

4. Once logged in, click on the profile icon on the top right to log out 
   or see user profile details.
